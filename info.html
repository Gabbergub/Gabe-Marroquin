A self-taught artist from Orange County, New York, who has dedicated 24 years to honing his craft with pencil and charcoal. His artistic journey commenced with the world of cartooning during his formative years, transitioning seamlessly into the intricate art of portrait drawing by the age of 18.

At the age of six, Gabe's passion for art was ignited by a gifted drawing of a beloved 'Pokemon' character, Mewtwo. Since that pivotal moment, art and drawing have become his drive, fueling an unyielding creative fervor.

Presently, Gabe is immersed in his studies of Communication Design at Parsons School of Design. While pursuing his academic endeavors, he remains committed to nurturing and expanding his artistic repertoire, anticipating the fusion of design principles with his passion for art.

While he is not immersed in his studies he works as a Teaching Assistant teaching Drawing and cartooning for ages 5-9 year olds at the 92NY.
